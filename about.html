Artist Statement

As an artist, Layne Hunt (b. 2002, Columbia SC) is driven by the desire to capture the beauty and movement of life in her art. Through the use of bold colors and delicate brushstrokes, Hunt creates a sense of atmosphere and emotion in each piece. Hunt's paintings are not meant to reproduce reality, but to evoke memories and emotions through the use of color and composition. Each brushstroke is a moment in time, layered together to create a sense of depth and history. The final result is a body of work that is both intimate, diverse, and thought-provoking. Hunt's paintings invite the viewer to experience the world in a new way and to find their own meaning in the art. The purpose of Hunt's painting is to create an emotional connection with the viewer and to share the beauty of life.
